Some more information

What is a credit Loan?

The loan of an amount of money whose debt is repaid, by the debtor to the creditor, in installments, over an agreed upon length of time and where predefined financial conditions are applied.

What makes a good credit loan?

First and foremost, a good loan answers your needs and considers your financial reality both in terms of installment duration and the conditions applied. These overall conditions should be balanced and measured. The experience of your credit specialist who will offer you a healthy plan which is useful and flexible should also be considered. Everything needs to be handled transparently. Lastly, organizations should apply data protection guidelines and build a trustworthy relationship with you.

Why take out a loan?

In our view, a loan can support you in realizing your projects, in making your dreams come true. You might also just need an influx of money for cashflow reasons. Each situation is unique, each project has its merit. Whether you need a personal loan or a professional loan, we will advise you with your best interests top of mind and with a focus on offering the best conditions available.

Why Swiss Finance?

Because we offer balanced loans which are useful and flexible in their application. Swiss Finance has streamlined the loan request process so that you have easy access to pertinent information. Swift and easy. We focus on building a relationship based on trust and apply balanced and transparent conditions for your loan. Trust is also the backbone of our relationships with the biggest banks in Switzerland. It is what allows us to have the best rates and overall conditions available on the market. Our online platform offers easy navigation and a swift and simple request process. Our specialists are based in Switzerland and reply within 24 hours to incoming loan requests. We stay at your side from your loan application to the transfer of funds to your bank account. With you every step of the way; That is what a trustworthy relationship is all about.

Our insight?

Go for quality. Go for transparency and choose a partner focused on making your projects a reality. Rely on specialists known for their expertise and whose mission it is to offer the best conditions available on the market. Always keeping the following at heart: simplicity, flexibility, speed and trust!
